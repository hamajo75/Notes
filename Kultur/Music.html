The 7 modes (C-major)

If we take the notes of C-major and start from each note we get 7 new scales (modes).

Structure 

W    W    H    W    W    W    H - C Ionian            C     Dm    Em   F     G     Am    B°
2    3    4    5    6    7    8                       Cmaj7 Dm7   Em7  Fmaj7 G7    Am7   Bm7b5

W    H    W    W    W    H    W - D Dorian            Dm Em F  G  Am B° C
2    b3   4    5    6    b7   8

H    W    W    W    H    W    W - E Phrygian          Em F  G  Am B° C  Dm
b2   b3   4    5    b6   b7   8   note: b2

W    W    W    H    W    W    H - F Lydian            F  G  Am B° C  Dm Em
2    3    #4   5    6    7    8   note: augmented 4

W    W    H    W    W    H    W - G Mixolydian        G  Am B° C  Dm Em F
2    3    4    5    6    b7   8

W    H    W    W    H    W    W - A Aeolian           Am B° C  Dm Em F  G
2    b3   4    5    b6   b7   8

H    W    W    H    W    W    W - B Locrian           B° C  Dm Em F  G  Am
b2   b3   4    b5   b6   b7   8

And of course we can use the structures and start those scales from C.

Notes:

  • Fourth & Fifth are almost always perfect (except Lydian).
